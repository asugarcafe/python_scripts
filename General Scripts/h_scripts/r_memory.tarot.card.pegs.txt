The suit of cups are usually associated with emotions, and correspond with the astrological element of water. 
The suit of cups associated Zodiac signs are Pisces, Cancer, and Scorpio.
The suit of swords are usually associated with intellectual activity, and correspond with the astrological element of air. 
The suit of swords associated Zodiac signs are Gemini, Libra and Aquarius.
The suit of wands are usually associated with the career, and correspond with the astrological element of fire. 
The suit of wands associated Zodiac signs are Leo, Aries and Sagittarius.
The suit of pentacles are usually associated with the fiancee and material aspects, and correspond with the astrological element of earth. 
The suit of pentacles associated Zodiac signs are Taurus, Capricorn and Virgo.
